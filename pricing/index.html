Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All our paid plans come with a 14-day, no-obligation free trial. You can explore all the features of your chosen plan and cancel anytime before the trial ends without being charged.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. Staxy is a monthly subscription with no long-term contracts. You can cancel your plan at any time from your account dashboard, no questions asked.

Are there any hidden fees or commissions?

None. We believe in transparent, flat-rate pricing. We charge a simple monthly fee. We do not take any commission on your upsell revenue. Standard payment processing fees from Stripe or Xendit will still apply.

Do I need any technical skills to set this up?

Not at all! Staxy was designed for busy villa owners, not tech experts. Our builder is incredibly simple to use. If you can write an email, you can build a beautiful guest app with Staxy.
